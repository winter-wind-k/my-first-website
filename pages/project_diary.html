Galea Garden is an interactive scrollytelling site showcasing medicinal plants used across the world for headache relief


I am the lead visual designer for this project. My tasks are as follows:

  • Assemble moodboards and reference images for plants, scenes, and garden elements for a cohesive aesthetic
  • Create concept sketches for illustrated elements and key scenes in the story
  • Design a style guide for main UI elements, including typeface, cards, buttons, and other interactive elements

I am also responsible for sound design (more on that later). My secondary roles include storyboard support, animation, and web development
