Tech Experiments

Ideation

While ideating, I sketched a cheeky little clown who was kicking their legs back and forth. I wanted to animate this to add as a decorative element to my site, for a bit more flavour. I took to procreate to trace over the original sketch.

Creation

I used strategies we learned in the 2D animation class last semester to develop a nice easing to the leg kicks. I.e. I created a timechart (more of a space chart but whatever). The blue ticks indicate where the feet will be. After I drew each of those feet in the arc, I duplicated the animation and switched the layering of the legs on each level so it would make sense.


I call that the Peter Leynes Special!

Implementation

Implementation was super easy. All you gotta do is put in an image. Literally that easy. I uploaded a transparent background gif from Procreate and put the pathway into the html and BOOM! clown city!
